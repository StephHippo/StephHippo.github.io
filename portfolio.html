The Jolly Advisor

advise.case.edu

Originally started as a senior project at Case Western, The Jolly Advisor has since been open sourced and turned over to CWRU's Hacker Society. After being frustrated with the speed and usability of CWRU's class registration system, The Jolly Advisor was created to help students search for classes and plan out their education in a way that made sense. Built with Ruby on Rails and deployed with Docker, anyone can contribute.
